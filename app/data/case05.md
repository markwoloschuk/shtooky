[paragraph]
The city of San Francisco told us we had illegal construction on our property. We had done nothing wrong. We were required to fix it anyway. The fix became something unexpected.

[pullquote]
I never studied design. I just did it. <And eventually I did it better.>

[paragraph]
Multi-year saga. Vindictive HOA president. Forgotten building complaint. Disconnected city databases. Permit jail before we'd unpacked. Required: gate with hinged bottom panel, 75% transparency per city code, car clearance in a narrow driveway. Two people with competing aesthetics who both had to love it.[br]Sketches, mockups, architect collaborator (Luc) who solved the hinge problem, 3D clearance model, After Effects color iteration tool so non-technical partner could explore palette herself. Stained glass circles. Colors pulled from the neighborhood.[br]I never studied design. I just did it. And eventually I did it better. This gate is what that looks like.

Hard.[br]Quick storyboard. Trebuchet instead of potato cannon. Key insight: flip the set 90 degrees, drop the printer straight down, let gravity do what a throw never could. Co-directed on set, got in front of the camera because it was funnier with two people, started the edit that night. Shot worked. Client loved it. Skip-level executive killed it.
